## Constraint at end of v2

**Tester** (median 1650s, n=2, trend: 1200s → 2100s). Shift from architect (v1 constraint).

Cause identified: slice 002 tester ran an exhaustive game-tree validation that duplicated the engineer's test suite. This is scope creep into algorithmic correctness, not customer-outcome validation.

## What changed between v2 and v3

1. **Tester scope clarified** — tester validates customer-observable outcomes through the public surface only. Algorithmic/exhaustive correctness is owned by the engineer's test suite; the tester confirms it passed (runs `pytest`) but does not re-implement the exhaustive check. This is the primary exploit of the tester constraint.
2. **Deploy event fix** — orchestrator logs the deploy event immediately when tester passes, before dispatching documenter; format confirmed against the script's expectations.
3. **per-project.md discipline** — orchestrator updates `dora/per-project.md` at the end of each slice retro, not delegated to engineer.
