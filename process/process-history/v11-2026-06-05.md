## Why superseded (v11 → v12)

Two consecutive smoke failures (s002 and s003) are now a pattern. The underlying cause is fragile smoke selectors — using button count/text exclusion rather than stable aria-label selectors. §22 was too narrow (route rewiring only); it missed the "new button added to existing screen" case. v12 broadens §22 and adds §23 (stable selector mandate for smoke tests).
